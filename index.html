How UniRider Works

UniRider connects students for convenient and eco-friendly campus travel. Whether youre heading to class, an event, or the library — we make ride-sharing safe, simple, and sustainable.

Step 1: Sign Up

Create your UniRider account using your verified college email. Set up your profile with your institution and preferred routes.

Step 2: Find or Offer

Search for rides going your way or offer your own. Instantly match with other students heading along similar routes.

Step 3: Connect & Confirm

Check profiles, view ratings, and confirm your ride. Coordinate easily using in-app chat or contact options.

Step 4: Ride & Save

Travel comfortably with peers, share costs, and reduce your carbon footprint while enjoying stress-free commuting.

About UniRider

UniRider is a modern bike and ride-sharing platform designed exclusively for campuses and young professionals. Our mission is to make campus mobility smarter, faster, and greener — helping students connect, commute, and collaborate effortlessly. With real-time tracking, secure payments, and verified users, UniRider ensures a safe, sustainable, and cost-effective ride experience.
